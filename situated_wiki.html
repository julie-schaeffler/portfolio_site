Feminist theory has been calling for a critically situated perspective on knowledge since Donna Haraway. Wikipedia has also claimed to be ‚Äúthe sum of all knowledge‚Äù, a apparently objective point of view.

This project analyses this tension: How is feminist knowledge constructed and treated differently in various language versions?

research questions

  1. What are the quantitative differences between language versions of feminist articles on Wikipedia, and are there epistemic patterns that can be identified from them?
  2. What qualitative differences can be seen in selected Wikipedia articles between language versions in a feminist context?
